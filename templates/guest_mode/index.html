About Program

JB Fest is the annual cultural extravaganza hosted by Jai Bharath College of Management and Technology, Arakkappady, Perumbavoor, Ernakulam. This vibrant celebration brings together students, faculty, and the local community to showcase their talents, foster creativity, and build a stronger sense of camaraderie. JB Fest is a melting pot of diverse cultural expressions, featuring a wide range of events and competitions, including music, dance, drama, art, literary events, and more. It provides a platform for aspiring artists and performers to showcase their skills, learn from experienced professionals, and interact with like-minded individuals.
